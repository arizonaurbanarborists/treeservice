When Do I Need Tree Removal?

We have a strong sense of tree preservation, but there are times when trees need to go. These are some of the most common reasons why tree removal in Arizona may become necessary:

Dead or Dying Trees: Falling poses dangers to people and property.

Storm Damage: Arizona has a serious monsoon season that can topple trees. It enhances the danger of falling.

Disease: Infections like contagious diseases may require removing the trees so that other plants don't get infected.

Property Safety: Trees around homes, power lines, or other structures growing close may require removal for safety purposes.

The Unique Challenges in Arizona Tree Landscape

This is where our experience in high desert climates benefits your tree care; we understand the challenges and have devised unique ways of servicing this exact area.

Drought: Arizona experiences drought more frequently than any other state, which hurts trees and makes them more susceptible to pests and diseases. Services like water management keep your trees well-hydrated throughout the year.

Heat Stress: In an Arizona sun, intense heat can cause extreme damage to trees with cracked bark or even death. Strategy services include mitigating the influence of heat stress on your trees.
